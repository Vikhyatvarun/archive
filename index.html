Thoughts 08 February 2026
Why we archive things
If it isn't saved locally, it doesn't exist. Relying on the cloud is a mistake. This website is my local backup. The internet is ephemeral - websites go down, services shut down, companies delete old content without warning. We've seen it happen over and over again. GeoCities gone. Google Reader gone. Countless blogs and forums lost to time. The only way to truly preserve something is to keep your own copy. That's why I maintain this archive. Every link, every project, every thought saved here is insurance against digital decay.
